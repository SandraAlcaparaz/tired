The 50s diner originally opened in the early 1950's where it took it's famous name. The secret recipes were passed on to their family name until today. The owners of this diner today kept the tradition and originality of their infrustructure and menu. They did not change any of the styles, decorations and recipes of their diner and their food because they wanted to keep their tradition and culture alive. With it's vintage and retro style, the customers of today's generation are sureley euphoric about the ambiance of the diner. Plus, the food that we serve give them a taste back from the past but we guarantee that even though we serve retro diner style food, we still create new styles on our dishes that will make you fall in love to the past and the future.

THE HISTORY OF THE 50s MENU

The 50s diner is very well known for their breakfast menu. You can taste the all-American breakfast in one bite with various ingredients and dishes. Soldiers in the early 50s used to gather themselves here at the diner to eat our famous meals. Famous artists like Paul Anka and James Dean visited the 50s diner various times to enjoy our food. Even though the retro diner is very famous because of their early breakfast menu and service, the diner is still open for business 24/7 serving breakfast, lunch, snacks and dinner.



The diner has it's own parking lot that the customers can use. The whole diner is secured with cctv cameras and two security guards to make sure that the customers and their belongings are secured. We can guarantee the safety and convenience of your stay while eating at our establishment. The 50s diner is already rated as a 5 star restaurant by the critics. In here, you can enjoy your delicious food with some entertainment from our jukeboxes, televisions and mini arcade. While spending some quality time anytime with yourself and your family and friends there's nothing to worry about your safety and belongings because the diner is highly secured.
